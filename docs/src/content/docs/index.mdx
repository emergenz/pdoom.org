**crowd-cast** is a desktop agent that records paired **screencast video** and
**keyboard/mouse input** for the applications you choose, building an open
dataset of real human computer work for research. It runs quietly in your menu
bar (macOS) or system tray, captures only the apps you select, pauses when you
step away, and uploads finished segments in the background.

It's a single binary with [libobs](https://github.com/obsproject/obs-studio)
embedded, so there's nothing else to install.

<LinkButton href="/docs/crowd-cast/setup/" variant="secondary">First-run setup</LinkButton>

## Get crowd-cast running in under five minutes

<Steps>

1. [Install](/docs/crowd-cast/install/) the app for your platform.
2. Walk through the [first-run setup](/docs/crowd-cast/setup/): grant permissions, pick the apps to record, and sign in.
3. You're recording. Manage everything from [the tray menu](/docs/crowd-cast/tray/).

</Steps>
