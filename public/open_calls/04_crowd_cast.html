Get paid to record your work.

●live · remote · $300 per month

crowd-cast is our desktop app for recording long-horizon computer-use data. The live dashboard shows current collection progress.

We pay accepted participants $300 per month to record open-source work. Eligible work includes research, engineering, design, editing, and other long-horizon computer tasks.

To participate, apply through the signup form. If accepted, you install crowd-cast, choose which applications can be recorded, and keep working as usual.

How it works
  • 01Apply through the signup form.
  • 02If accepted, install crowd-cast on macOS, Windows, or Linux.
  • 03On first launch, you choose which applications get recorded. Everything outside that list is automatically excluded.
  • 04We capture five-minute segments and upload to a private S3 bucket. The local copy is deleted on each segment boundary.
